Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the AquaPause application ("the App"). Your privacy is deeply respected, and this policy outlines exactly how your data is handled when you use the App.

1. Camera Usage

AquaPause requires access to your device's camera to detect water consumption by scanning your drinking habits in real-time.

  • Local Processing Only: All camera processing happens strictly on your local device.
  • No Recording: The App does not record, take photos, or save any video data to your device's storage.
  • No Transmission: We do not transmit, upload, or share any camera feed or video data to any external servers, third parties, or cloud services.

2. Accessibility Service

AquaPause uses Android's Accessibility Service to monitor which app is currently in the foreground on your device. This is used solely to enforce your selected app usage limits during active timer sessions.

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit information about which apps you use.
  • On-Device Only: All app monitoring logic runs entirely on your device and is never shared externally.

3. Local Data

AquaPause saves your settings, selected apps, and timer preferences locally on your device using encrypted local storage. This data never leaves your phone and is not linked to your identity. If you uninstall the App or clear its data, all saved information will be permanently deleted.

4. Subscriptions and Billing

AquaPause offers optional premium features via a subscription managed through Google Play Billing and RevenueCat. Subscription and payment data is handled entirely by Google Play and RevenueCat according to their respective privacy policies. We do not store your payment information.

5. Third-Party Services

AquaPause uses RevenueCat solely for managing in-app subscriptions. No third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services are integrated into the App.

6. Children's Privacy

AquaPause does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The App does not collect, store, or transmit personal data of any kind.

7. Changes to This Policy

If future features change how data is handled, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly and you will be notified within the App.
